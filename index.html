About the Book

History is not always written in ink – it often survives in memory, in spoken words passed down through generations, in fading documents, and in the quiet pride of communities that remember what others have chosen to forget. Voice of Barak Valley is an effort to record, preserve, and honour the complex socio-political journey of the Barak Valley and its people.

About the Author

Muazzaz Ahmed Choudhury

Also known as Faaiz Choudhury – law student, writer, and music producer. Rooted in the Barak Valley's history, he writes to amplify voices and preserve memory. Through this work, he hopes to bring Southern Assam's narrative to the national conversation.
