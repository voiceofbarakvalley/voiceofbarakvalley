Voice of Barak Valley (Assam)

Unheard Stories, Unspoken Truths from the Soul of Southern Assam by Muazzaz Ahmed Choudhury (Faaiz)

About the Author

Muazzaz Ahmed Choudhury, known as Faaiz, is a writer, researcher, and passionate voice from Assam's Barak Valley. With deep cultural ties to the region, he aims to amplify the voices and struggles that often go unheard in mainstream narratives.

Why This Book Was Written

This book was written to shine a light on the neglected yet rich cultural, political, and social life of the Barak Valley. It serves as a tribute to a region often misunderstood and misrepresented, offering a platform for its people, stories, and resilience.
