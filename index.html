About the Book

History is not always written in ink—it survives in memories, spoken words, and fading documents. Voice of Barak Valley is a tribute to the socio-political journey of Southern Assam, its people, and the voices that shaped its identity.

About the Author

Muazzaz Ahmed Choudhury

Also known as Faaiz Choudhury – law student, writer, and music producer. He brings forth the untold legacy of the Barak Valley with a mission to preserve memory and identity.
