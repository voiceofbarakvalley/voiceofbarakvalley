Voice of Barak Valley

Unheard stories and unspoken truths from the heart of Southern Assam

By Muazzaz Ahmed Choudhury (Faaiz Choudhury)

About the Book

History is not always written in ink – it often survives in memory, in spoken words passed down through generations, in fading documents, and in the quiet pride of communities that remember what others have chosen to forget. Voice of Barak Valley is an attempt to record, preserve, and honour the complex socio-political journey of the Barak Valley and its people.

About the Author

Muazzaz Ahmed Choudhury, also known as Faaiz Choudhury, is a law student, writer, and music producer with a passion for uncovering forgotten histories. Through his work, he strives to bring the Barak Valley's cultural and historical narrative to the national forefront.
