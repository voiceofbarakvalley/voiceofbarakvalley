About the Book

History is not always written in ink—it survives in memories, spoken words, and fading documents. Voice of Barak Valley aims to preserve the socio-political journey of Southern Assam, capturing its struggles, contributions, and unspoken truths that deserve a place in history.

About the Author

Muazzaz Ahmed Choudhury (Faaiz Choudhury) is a law student, writer, and music producer dedicated to bringing the forgotten narratives of Barak Valley to the forefront.
