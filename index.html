Voice of Barak Valley (Assam)

Unheard Stories, Unspoken Truths from the Soul of Southern Assam

This book, written by Muazzaz Ahmed Choudhury (Faaiz), explores the socio-cultural, political, and emotional landscape of Barak Valley. It is a cinematic yet authentic attempt to capture voices that have long remained in silence.

About the Author

Muazzaz Ahmed Choudhury, also known as Faaiz, is a law student, writer, and electronic music producer. A native of Barak Valley, Assam, he has authored various articles and is deeply connected to the region's heritage and transformation. This book is a tribute to his homeland and its people.
