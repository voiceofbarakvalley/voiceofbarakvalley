Voice of Barak Valley

Unheard stories and unspoken truths from the heart of Southern Assam

About the Book

History is not always written in ink—it survives in memories and voices passed through generations. Voice of Barak Valley is a tribute to the socio-political journey of Southern Assam, capturing its struggles, contributions, and cultural essence.

About the Author

Muazzaz Ahmed Choudhury (Faaiz Choudhury) is a law student, writer, and music producer dedicated to preserving the forgotten narratives of Southern Assam.
